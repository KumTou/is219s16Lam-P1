Sabrina Lam

Is a passionate and enthusiastic designer, whether it be in computers, visual art or music, hoping to earn her B.S. degree in Web and Information Systems from New Jersey's Institute of Technology in the spring of 2017.

Before pursuing a career in the technology industry, Sabrina was originally a music education major attr Rutgers University's Mason Gross School of the Arts. She is knowledgeable in various instruments and genres of music, having composed some of her own.

Besides music, Sabrina is also interested in visual design, as well as computers (especially games). Before designing using electronical devices, she has always been sketching various designs using pencil and paper, and has experience in using ceramics at her part-time job as a studio manager in a small pottery painting studio.

With experience in both technology and the arts, Sabrina takes great care in what goes into her projects, putting her own style while still making it appealing to others.
